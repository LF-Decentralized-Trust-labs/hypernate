# Code of Conduct

Hypernate is part of [LF Decentralized Trust](https://www.lfdecentralizedtrust.org/) and follows
the **LF Decentralized Trust Code of Conduct**.

All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to read and abide by it:

**➡️ <https://www.lfdecentralizedtrust.org/code-of-conduct>**

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it. You can contact the
LF Decentralized Trust community team at:

- mdolan@linuxfoundation.org
- angela@linuxfoundation.org

You may also reach out to the project [maintainers](MAINTAINERS.md). All reports will be handled
discreetly.
